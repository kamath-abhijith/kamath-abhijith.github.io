About Me

I am Abijith J Kamath. I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. I work in the areas of signal/image processing - in particular sub-Nyquist sampling theory and inverse problems.

I was born on the 17th of April, in Mangalore, India. Here, I spent my schooling and university years until I got my Bachelors in Technology in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the National Institute of Technology, Karnataka.

I am also interested in pure mathematics. I have an identity crisis - am I an electrical engineer in signal processing or a bad mathematician looking for practical applications to solve. I wish to meet Mr Nicholas Bourbaki some day to help me make my mind :P I also enjoy travelling and photography, and I am a die-hard Manchester United supporter!
