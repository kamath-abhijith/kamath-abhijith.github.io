## Time-based Sampling

Time-based sampling is a new and emerging idea in the signal processing and analogue electronics where the analogue-to-digital converter is replaced by a time-encoding machine (TEM), which are asynchronous real-time circuits that records time-instants where certain pre-defined events occur corresponding to the input signal. This is an unconventional sampling scheme! This paper addresses the problem of sampling and reconstruction of FRI signals from such time-encoded measurements.
